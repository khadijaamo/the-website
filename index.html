First of all, forgive yourself. If you made something and it didn't work out, let it go. Remember that you're nothing but a beginner - even if you've been working on your craft for fifty years. We are all just beginners here, and and we shall all die beginners. So let it go. Forget about the last project, and go searching with an open heart for the next one.

From Big Magic by Elizabeth Gilbert
